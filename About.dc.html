Who We Are

We're Your Digital Partner, Not Your Vendor

Cortanatech Solutions exists to bridge the gap between mission and technology — for organizations that are changing the world but don't have a tech team to back them up.

Our Mission

Helping Organizations Focus on What Matters Most

Too many churches, schools, and nonprofits are held back by technology — not empowered by it. They spend time managing broken websites, wrestling with email setups, or waiting on overpriced agencies. We exist to change that.

Cortanatech Solutions was built to give mission-driven organizations access to practical, professional digital infrastructure — affordably, and with a partner who actually understands their world.

Our Approach

We Come Alongside You — Not At You

We don't hand down technical jargon or disappear after delivery. We walk with you through the process, explain what we're building and why, and make sure your team feels confident using whatever we create.

Whether you're a pastor, a school administrator, or a first-time founder — you don't need to speak tech to work with us. We translate both ways.

Partnership over vendor-ship
We're in it with you, not just for you. Your success is our success.
Transparency at every step
No surprises in scope, timeline, or pricing. We communicate plainly and consistently.
Practical over flashy
We build what you need, not what looks impressive in a portfolio. Functional is the goal.
